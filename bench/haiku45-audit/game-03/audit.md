## Finding 1 — Take Resource action granted 2 resources instead of 1
- Step/round: R8 · Investigation, log.md lines 459–465 (log.jsonl seq 399–405)
- Severity: pro-player
- Rule: RR "Draw Resource Action" — the investigator "takes one resource from the token bank." The engine's own option label agrees: "Take resource (gain 1)".
- Evidence: Status at the decision (seq 398) shows `res0`. One action is spent ("Spent 1 action: Take resource."), the Mob Enforcer attack of opportunity resolves, then TWO separate "gained 1 resource" events fire (seq 404 and 405), and the next status (seq 407) shows `res2`. Nothing in play grants extra resources: Hallway/Guest Hall have no such text, Hospital Debts only moves resources out of the pool, the Breaking the Wall test grants nothing on success, and no elder sign was revealed (Skids's elder-sign resource effect requires that token). Net: the player received 1 illegal resource, which he then banked on Hospital Debts. The both-gains-after-the-AoO ordering suggests the AoO interrupt/resume path (cf. adjudication #8) re-applied the action's effect, but whatever the mechanism, one action yielded two resources.

## Finding 2 — Frozen in Fear's end-of-turn Forced test resolved before the post-final-action player window
- Step/round: R8 · Investigation, log.md line 466 vs lines 479–484 (log.jsonl seq 406 vs 416–420)
- Severity: unclear
- Rule: Frozen in Fear (01164): "**Forced** – At the end of your turn: Test [willpower] (3). If you succeed, discard Frozen in Fear." Per the RR/FAQ investigation-phase timing chart — and as established in adjudication #6 for this engine — a player window remains open after the investigator's last action, still *during* the turn; the turn ends (and end-of-turn Forced effects fire) only after that window closes. Skids (01003): "[fast] **During your turn**, spend 2 resources: You may take an additional action this turn. (Limit once per turn.)"
- Evidence: The Frozen in Fear test starts at seq 406 immediately after the Take Resource action's resolution (seq 405), with no intervening player window; the `fast-window-inv_end` window is only presented at seq 416/420, *after* the test has resolved (failure, FiF stays). The turn was not forcibly ended (Take Resource was simply his last action), so per the RR order the during-turn window had to precede the turn's end. Skids had 2 resources and had not used his once-per-turn extra-action ability that turn, so buying a 4th action was legal in that window — the engine ended his turn (firing the FiF test) before ever offering it, and any window offered after the end-of-turn trigger cannot legally contain during-turn options. This is the same window/forced-ordering class as adjudications #6/#7, surfacing on the Frozen in Fear end-of-turn trigger.
