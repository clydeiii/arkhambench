## Finding 1 — Dark Memory’s resource cost was not paid before its attack of opportunity
- Step/round: R8 Investigation, log lines 431-438
- Severity: display
- Rule: RR Appendix I: pay costs, then attacks of opportunity resolve; RR Attack of Opportunity: attacks occur “immediately after all costs of initiating the action that provoked the attack have been paid”; Dark Memory costs 2 resources.
- Evidence: At line 431 Agnes has 10 resources. Lines 433-436 choose and spend an action to play Dark Memory, then Ghoul from the Depths makes the attack of opportunity. Line 438 still shows 10 resources. The 2-resource event cost should already have been paid before that attack resolved.
