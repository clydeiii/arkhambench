## Finding 1 — DB setup doom for 5 got-away cultists may be one over the guide's bracket

- **Step:** Return to the Devourer Below setup (run c-show-terra-wendy-3, log line "Setup placed 3 doom from cultists who got away" — 5 names).
- **Observed engine mapping** (across all campaigns in the repo): 4 got away → 2 doom, 5 → 3, 6 → 3 — i.e. ceil(n/2).
- **Concern:** the core campaign guide's Devourer Below setup table, as I recall its printed text, brackets as *3 or fewer → 1 doom, 4–5 → 2 doom, 6 → 3 doom* — under which 5 got-away should place **2** doom, not 3. The discrepancy is material here: agenda 1a (threshold 4) started at 3/4 and advanced on the first mythos doom of R2, costing Wendy roughly a full agenda stage of tempo. The same 5→3 placement appears in at least seven other showcase campaigns.
- **Caveats, stated plainly:** I could not verify the printed table — the docs_agent references don't contain it and access to data/rules was denied in this session; this is Return to NotZ, and the Return guide could legitimately rescale the bracket (a "3–4 → 2, 5–6 → 3" or per-2-rounded-up reading fits every observed data point). Ledger entry 45 adjudicated *the existence* of setup doom as correct-per-table but the case it examined wasn't necessarily a 5-cultist one. This finding therefore needs one check of the Return DB setup page: if the Return guide keeps the core 4–5 bracket, the engine over-places 1 doom for exactly the 5-cultist case; if it rescales, close this as NOT A BUG and the campaign layer is fully clean.
